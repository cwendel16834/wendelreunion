Where to meet at the Picnic

We can meet at the covered front porch of the church at 1:00 PM to visit informally with our Wendel relatives. These would be the descendants of Peter Jacob and Barbara (Braden) Wendel.

We will try to have a Wendel Family registration booklet at the church entrance, so you can sign in when you arrive to let others know which relatives are at the picnic.

Come early and enjoy the all-day annual church picnic. Mass begins at 10:30 am. You can buy BBQ plates (11 am – 2 pm) and other refreshments to support the church community, with dining in the NEW HALL or the outdoor Pavillion. There will be entertainment for all ages--bring your kids!

P.S. The traditional Wendel Reunion will be Saturday October 11, 2014 in Wharton, Texas.

How to get to the Mentz Picnic

  • The Mentz Community is about 8 miles northeast of Columbus, Texas.
  • From Interstate 10, Take Exit #704 for Farm to Market (FM) 949.
  • Drive North on FM 949 for only about 1/2 mile.
  • (Do not follow the sharp curve to the right, where FM 949 continues to the east.)
  • Drive ahead and towards the left onto Frelsburg Road for about 1-1/2 miles.
  • When you reach the “T” intersection with Mentz Road (CR 61), you will see St. Roch’s Church ahead and towards the right.
  • The nearest towns to Mentz shown on Texas state maps are Alleyton and Cat Spring.
